Have you tried using any of the insight and techniques for growing trust and sincerity? How did it go?

I think the ability to come into a situation with others whereby you assume the best first and trust first is extremely valuable. People can usually detect at least on a subconsious level whether you are at ease with them or not and it tends to be a self fulfilling prophecy. I try to do this as often as I can when I meet new people and when I do it seems to have a better result.

Try empathic listening - how did it go?

I enjoyed this exercise, it is often interesting to quiet down and be observant of what the other person feels and is trying to say.

How did you find the loving kindness meditation?

While at first Skeptical, I found this exercise to be enjoyable and a reminder that others are in a similar situation to yourself. Often we think our problems are unique and special to ourselves but in reality this is rarely the case.
