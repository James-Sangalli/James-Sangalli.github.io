What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

Self awareness and mindfulness are similar in that they both require a degree of awareness of the situation and how you are reacting to it

Try at least one of the self awareness practices from the chapter (Body Scan, Journaling). What was your experience of it? How did you feel?

The activity I tried was placing full attention on the emotions which pop up. For example, today I had problems with my wifi driver on my computer This created a feeling of anger and annoyance but instead of allowing it to dictate my actions I observed it until it floated away.

What did you take away from the "My Emotions are Not me" section?

The main thing I took away from this video section was that often we let emotions dictate our actions when we are in the grasp of one. How many times can we claim that we have acted irrational when feeling a surge of a strong emotions and later regret our short sightedness? I venture to say that all of us have done so and would benefit by being more aware of our temperment.
